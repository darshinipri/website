Maha Mrityunjaya Yagna


As the name of the Yagna itself suggest Mritunajam that literally means in Sanskrit “ Mrituam Jayati eti Mritunajayh”. It means the Yagna has the power to give victory over the critical situation even like death. The word Mrityormooksheya as depicted in Mantra, ensures that devotee or performer of this Yagna get Moksha i.e. freedom from a particular situation that you are entrapped in life. The life threatening situations like accidents, imprisonments, entangled in bad relationship where you are feeling suffocated or unable to find relief. Lord Shiva is known as the "giver" god or Tryambaka Deva (literally, "three-eyed Lord"). He is Mahadev, one of the Three Supreme Gods; we bow our head in devotion to the almighty Lord Shiva, who is the destroyer of all evils. Offering prayers to Lord Shiva through Maha Mritunjaya Yagna will help overcome difficult phases of life & relief from any kind of serious trouble

It help to recover from critical & miserable health condition, a relief from suffering caused by any sort of miscellaneous deadly & hazardous situation in life. With the blessings of Lord Shiva and Maha Mrityunjaya jaap, not only you will be able to get rid of complications but also you will feel confidence and strength in yourself to deal with all that comes up for you and your family in the future.
People going through the life threatening diseases/health issues even if there is fear of death or having some legal problem where prosecution or imprisonment is suspected or trapped in a bad relationship where there is no way to come out, then performing this Yagna would be great help to win over the situation.
