Husband & Wife Dispute


the analysis of potential disputes or challenges in a marriage and their relationships

The individual birth charts of both the husband and wife are examined. The positions of the sun, moon, planets, and other celestial bodies, as well as their aspects (angular relationships), can reveal personality traits, emotional needs, and communication styles. the compatibility between two people's birth charts. The aspects formed between planets in each spouse's chart can indicate areas of ease and tension in the relationship. For example, harmonious aspects may indicate compatibility, while challenging aspects might highlight areas of conflict.

Astrologers may also consider current planetary transits and progressions to provide insights into the timing of events or changes in the marriage. For example, they might highlight periods of increased tension or opportunities for resolution.Astrologers may offer suggestions for personal growth and self-awareness for both partners. These recommendations can include ways to work on individual issues that may be affecting the marriage.
Depending on the nature of the dispute and the client's needs, the astrologer might offer follow-up sessions to monitor progress, reassess the astrological influences, and provide ongoing support
