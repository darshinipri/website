Vastu


Vastu Shastra, often referred to simply as "Vastu," is an ancient Indian architectural and design philosophy that focuses on the layout, design, and orientation of buildings, structures, and spaces to create harmonious and balanced living environments. Vastu has its roots in Indian cultural and architectural traditions and is followed by many individuals and architects.

Some people believe in aligning the layout and design of their homes or spaces with their astrological charts. For example, the orientation of the entrance or the placement of specific rooms may be determined with consideration of a person's birth chart. strological insights can also guide the timing of Vastu-related activities, such as moving into a new home, performing Vastu rituals, or making structural changes to a property. Favorable astrological periods may be chosen for such activities.

Astrologers may recommend specific astrological remedies or practices to enhance the positive effects of Vastu principles or to mitigate negative influences. These remedies can include the use of gemstones, mantras, and specific rituals. When implementing Vastu principles, some practitioners may consider the astrological aspects of a space, including the positioning of celestial bodies, moon phases, and planetary transits, to ensure that the design and layout align with astrological principles.
