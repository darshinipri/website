Palmistry


Palmistry, also known as chiromancy, is the practice of interpreting the lines, shapes, and features of an individual's hands, specifically the palms, to gain insights into their personality, character, and potential future. It is considered a form of divination or pseudoscience.

Palmistry has been practiced in various forms across different cultures and regions for centuries, and it is a subject of interest to those who believe in metaphysical and mystical practices. It's important to note that palmistry is not a scientifically proven method for predicting an individual's future or character, and its accuracy is a matter of debate. It is often viewed as a form of entertainment or personal reflection rather than a legitimate means of divining the future.
